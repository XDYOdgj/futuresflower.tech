Enhon Drain Cleaning Bladder with Shut Off Valve

Built with a 3/4-inch shut off valve, it allows precise control of water pressure and flow, eliminating the hassle of stopping and restarting your water supply. Whether you're dealing with hair, soap scum, or grease buildup, this drain unclogger delivers efficient results in less time.

  • 3/4-inch shut off valve allows control over water flow

Whether you're a DIY enthusiast or a busy homeowner, this drain pipe cleaner is a must-have for your toolkit.
