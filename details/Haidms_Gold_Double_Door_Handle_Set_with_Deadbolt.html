Haidms Gold Double Door Handle Set with Deadbolt

Elevate the look and security of your doors with this high-quality hardware set. Featuring a radiant gold finish, it adds a touch of elegance to both modern and traditional settings. Crafted from heavy-duty zinc alloy, it ensures durability and resilience, even in high-traffic areas. The set includes a dummy handle and a keyed deadbolt, offering enhanced security without sacrificing style. The reversible handle lever makes installation easier, as you can choose the orientation that matches your door's opening direction. With an adjustable backset design (2-3/8" to 2-3/4"), it's compatible with a variety of door thicknesses and configurations. Perfect for residential or commercial use, this set is a reliable choice for securing your entry points while enhancing your home's curb appeal.
