Enhance Your Home Security with the Lockly Duo

The perfect blend of convenience and protection. Combine smart fingerprint and keypad entry with remote control functionality for effortless access and peace of mind.

Advanced Security Features

Featuring a Dual Lock System, the Lockly Duo offers two layers of protection, ensuring your home remains secure at all times. With Fingerprint Entry, Keypad Entry, and Smart Key access, you can unlock your door in seconds. The system also includes Anti-Peep Protection for enhanced privacy and PIN Genie Technology to automatically generate and shuffle PINs, keeping your access information safe from prying eyes.

Smart Convenience at Your Doorstep

Enjoy the peace of mind that comes with an Auto-Lock Mechanism, which automatically locks your door when you leave and unlocks it when you return. The Lockly Duo is compatible with your mobile device through its Remote App Control, allowing you to manage access from anywhere. Its SATIN Nickel finish adds a sleek, durable look, while the IP67 Weatherproofing ensures it withstands the elements and lasts for years.

Built to Last with Reliable Warranties

Designed for long-term use and performance, the Lockly Duo comes with a 5-Year Mechanical Warranty and a 2-Year Electronics Warranty. Whether you're staying in or traveling, it's the smart lock system that offers reliability, ease of use, and cutting-edge safety technology for your everyday needs.
